COMPANY PROFILE

AJ Research & Pharma (AJRP) is a research-driven branded-generic formulation manufacturing company bringing high quality pharmaceutical and consumer health products for societal well-being. AJRP strives to provide advanced pharmaceutical products for a wide range of disease segments. Our products meet the highest level of global quality standards.

Through our in-house research and development capabilities, AJRP has vertically integrated to develop high quality innovative products tailored to the growing needs of the community. Our research and development process and methodology are consistent with the stringent global quality standards instituted by highly regulated markets.

Our Vision

To become an innovation-driven world-class pharmaceutical and nutraceutical company providing the community with healthcare solutions — from prevention to treatment.

Our Mission

To promote a healthy community through continuous research and development of the highest quality products, improving not just health, but also overall quality of life.

WHAT WE STRIVE TO DO

At AJRP, we are committed to developing, manufacturing, and marketing high quality generic pharmaceuticals. We believe all customers taking our products, wherever they are located, are entitled to the highest quality products.

Our goal is to be at the forefront of our industry in bringing complex medicinal products to the markets we serve.

VSENSE® targets women who suffer from FSD. We are dedicated to building awareness about the negative effects that FSD can bring to women, while providing a solution through this carefully formulated product that is easily accessible to the market. We strive to assure that AJRP’s resources, expertise, and energy are focused on the opportunities that can bring the most benefit to all our customers. We then rely on great science and passionate execution to bring these opportunities to fruition.

Through VSENSE®, our mission is to improve the intimate lives of women suffering from FSD by developing an innovative treatment that addresses sexual problems commonly faced. VSENSE® is here to help women. Let’s help them TURN LIFE ON again.
